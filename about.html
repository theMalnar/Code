About Gregory

I've spent much of my professional career in fine dining kitchens. With this I've acquired a unique skillset that allows me to thrive under intense pressure while following recipes to the T, the ability to execute improvisational solutions to unforseen problems, and also communicate effectively, clearly, and meaningful to the entire team. These skills are what I bring with me in every job I take on, be it digital marketing, email campaign marketing, SEO analysis, graphic design, UI/UX design and research, or front end development. The ability to follow instructions, problemsolve (troubleshoot and debug), improvise (self-research), and communicate are what make me a great contender.

Currently I am freelancing for a Terpene Formulation Company and a Loan Brokerage company, doing graphic design and email marketing for both. I've also started my own business in reverse-wholseale that taps into the immensity of the Amazon market place. I seek to make Amazon and Email Marketing my primary sources of income, so that I can learn to design and code in leisure, and to pick the projects I want because they're meaningful to me, not because I need a job.
